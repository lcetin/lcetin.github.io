MEE104 Measurement&Evaluation Techniques

MEE104 is an active course in Mechatronics Engineering Program. It is offered every spring term for the first year students. The course language is English.
The participants are supposed to gain skills for using basic workshop measurements, to understand the statistical nature of measurement results and to be able carry out calibration procedures. The course includes theoritical presentations,recitations and laboratory experiments
The weekly schedule of the course is given in below table together with lecture notes. Please note that presentations and whiteboard examples are not avaialble in anyway unless you take notes during lectures

MEE310 Control Sytems

MEE310 is an active course in Mechatronics Engineering Program at IKCU. It is offered every spring term for the third year students. The course language is English.
The participants are supposed to gain skills for analysis, sythesis, and design of classical control systems The course includes theoritical presentations and projects.The reference book for course is "Control Systems Engineering,Norman S. Nise"

MEE212 Electric Circuits II

MEE212 is an active course in Mechatronics Engineering Program at IKCU. It is offered every spring term for the second year students. The course language is English.
The participants are supposed to gain skills for analysis, sythesis, and design of electric circuits The course includes theoritical presentations, lab work and projects.The reference book for course is "Electric Circuits (10th Edition) by W. Nilsson, Susan Riedel"
